Last Knight

Profile


Last Knight is a promising SWM artist, with what seems like a bright future. He is also a co-founder of the label having played a significant role in strategy development and implementation


MUSIC


His sound is very raw and tells the story of Soweto from Last Knight's point of view. Listening to his music, one can tell that hardships, betrayal and hustling, are all aspects that influence both his sounds as well as his childhood, yet he has managed to make it a sound that anyone can enjoy
