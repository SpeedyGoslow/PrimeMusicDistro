Tymless


Producer & Recording Artist



Profile


Mbulelo Mkontwana, also known as Tymless is a multi-instrumentalist, rapper, song-writer and record producer known for his progressive musical styles, timeless compositions and sentient song writing


MUSIC


Tymless has worked closely with fellow musician JayMar to create a distinctive premium sound, which they call Lo-fi West, the name is derived from LoFi hip hop infused with the infamous Kasi Lingo/Tsotsi taal which in turn refers to a form of slang spoken widely throughout South African townships
